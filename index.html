A local wedding registry for nontraditional couples

We believe that engaged couples who care about shopping local would like the opportunity to register for gifts from vendors in their community. Have & Hold is a wedding registry app that allows couples to register gifts with local businesses. Couples can find one of a kind gifts and experiences in their community that will make their wedding special.

Research

Survey Data

99% of respondents placed importance on shopping from local businesses.

78.6% of respondents indicated they were likely or very likely to choose a local registry if it existed .

The Problem

We observed that engaged couples think wedding registries are overly traditional and don’t fit their personality, lifestyle or values. Many couples feel uncomfortable asking for gifts and don’t want to be gifted material items they do not need.

How might we curate a registry that frees unconventional couples to register for gifts they truly want and feel good about asking for?
